Privacy Policy

This page explains what information JwithKP may collect through the website, how it is used for business communication, and how we handle inquiries responsibly.

Effective date: March 31, 2026

Information we collect

When you contact JwithKP through the website, email, phone, or WhatsApp, we may collect your name, company name, email address, phone number, project details, and any business information you choose to share with us.

How we use your information

We use submitted information to respond to your inquiry, understand your business requirement, prepare recommendations, estimate implementation scope, and follow up regarding consulting or delivery services requested by you.

What we do not do

We do not sell your personal or company information to third parties. We do not use your inquiry details for unrelated marketing offers. We aim to keep business communication relevant to the services you asked about.

Data sharing

Information may be shared only when reasonably required to operate communication channels or deliver requested services, such as with email, hosting, or collaboration providers. We keep such sharing limited to legitimate business use.

Data retention

We retain inquiry information only for as long as it is useful for handling your request, maintaining service communication, internal recordkeeping, or meeting legal and operational obligations.

Your choices

You may request that we update or delete personal information previously shared with us, subject to any legal or business recordkeeping requirement. You may also ask us to stop follow-up communication at any time.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact JwithKP at info@jwithkp.com or call +91 79829 12207.
