Frequently Asked Questions

Answers Before You Start

Most clients want clarity on timelines, downtime, support, and whether open-source platforms are the right fit. Here are the conversations we have most often.

How do you decide between open-source and commercial solutions?

We recommend based on business fit, not ideology. We look at budget, internal skills, support expectations, compliance needs, and the operational risk of each option before finalizing the stack.

Will migration cause downtime for our team?

Most projects are planned in phases so user impact stays low. We usually schedule cutovers after hours, test rollback options, and communicate clearly before anything production-facing changes.

Do you work with our existing IT team?

Yes. We can lead the full implementation or work alongside your current team for architecture, rollout support, documentation, and handover so they stay confident after go-live.

What happens after deployment is complete?

You can choose structured handover, periodic health checks, or ongoing managed support. The goal is to leave you with a stable environment and a clear plan for the next stage of growth.
